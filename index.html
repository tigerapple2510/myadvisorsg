DRIP vs Cash — At Retirement

📖 DRIP vs Cash — which should you choose?
🔄 DRIP — Reinvest dividends
Every dividend buys more shares automatically. No cash received along the way. Compounds faster — bigger portfolio at retirement.

Best for: wealth builders who don't need income yet.
💵 Cash — Receive dividends monthly
Dividends paid to you in cash from day one. Portfolio grows slower. Regular monthly income now, smaller end portfolio.

Best for: retirees or anyone who needs income today.
💡 Smart strategy: Use DRIP while working to build the largest possible portfolio, then switch to Cash at retirement. A larger DRIP base generates more monthly income at retirement than a Cash portfolio would.
